A little help: how you can start drawing

STEP ONE

Search inspo

You can look search for pictures/images that feels good to look at or some idea of what to draw, wich colors and shades, etc. Some peron?! some place?! some car?!

Once you find what kind of drawing you want to, you can keep going to the Second Step.

STEP TWO

Sketch

You can do some free sketch without any worries an then start to build it with some darker lines and shapes, going trough the ideas in your mind, bringing them to the paper.

Once you're done with your sketch, you can keep going to the Third Step.

STEP THREE

Lineart

It's time to build this sketch with lineart. It depends of your style of drawing, you can try some differents linearts untill you find the one. Now that you found your lineart, let's do it happen.

Once your lineart is ready, you can keep going to the Fourth Step.

STEP FOUR

The colors(or not)

We are almost finished, you just have to choose if you want color in your draw or if it will be just about lineart.


WE ARE FINISHED! YOU DID IT! CONGRATS!
